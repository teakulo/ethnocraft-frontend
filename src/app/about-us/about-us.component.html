Welcome to EthnoCraft

Exploring and preserving the beauty of Bosnian and Herzegovinian crafts.

Our Mission

At EthnoCraft, our mission is to showcase and promote the rich cultural heritage of Bosnia and Herzegovina by celebrating traditional crafts and artisans.

Facts About Our Country

  • Bosnia and Herzegovina is located in the Balkan region of Southeast Europe.
  • It is known for its stunning natural landscapes, including lush forests, crystal-clear rivers, and picturesque mountains.
  • The country has a diverse cultural heritage, with influences from various civilizations throughout history.
  • Bosnia and Herzegovina is famous for its traditional crafts, such as pottery, weaving, and woodcarving.
